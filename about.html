Hi!

I'm Lucija Babić, and I just graduated with a degree in Informatics from the Faculty of Informatics and Digital Technologies in Rijeka, Croatia.

My journey into the world of technology began with a simple love for creativity and computers. It fascinated me how ideas could come to life through design and just a few lines of code - and that feeling of seeing something evolve on screen still inspires me every day.

Throughout my studies, I worked on various digital projects - from web and mobile applications to animations and graphic design. I’ve learned to use different programming languages and tools, but through that journey I discovered that front-end and design are where I feel most at home.

I enjoy working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and design tools like Figma and Canva, combining functionality with clean, engaging visuals. What motivates me most is the process of creating something meaningful - from the first concept to the final detail. I love exploring colors, layouts, and user experience, always aiming to build websites that not only look good but also feel intuitive and accessible.

Outside of coding and design, I’m behind my Canon camera or DJI drone, capturing moments that inspire me. Photography helps me notice beauty in details and gives me original material for my web projects - from visuals to product photos for clients.

My goal is simple - to help brands and individuals turn their ideas into beautiful, functional, and meaningful digital experiences that connect creativity, purpose, and technology.
