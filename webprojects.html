RedCap – 3D Character Animation

This project combines 3D design, animation and front-end development to represent a brand I created – RedCap. Using Spline, I shaped and customized the character to match the brand’s identity, adding the signature red hat, the logo I designed, and creating a camera orbit animation around the model.

I also coded and developed the website where this 3D animation is showcased, ensuring it aligns with the visual and interactive tone of my brand. This project merges my skills in web development, design, and motion into one cohesive creative expression – RedCap.

V&R Cleaning – Full Website Development & Design Implementation

For this project, I developed the entire website for V&R Cleaning, a small cleaning company. I worked with the existing logo and extracted a color palette from their single Instagram post, which surprisingly matched their favorite colors. I designed the layout in Figma, prepared all content including texts and images (with limited material provided), and then coded the site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

As I progressed, I adapted the project to WordPress, creating dynamic fields so the client could easily update prices and content. This required converting the static HTML/CSS/JS structure into PHP templates compatible with WordPress. The project showcases problem-solving, design-to-code workflow, and implementing interactive elements while keeping the client’s needs in mind.

The site was never published due to client circumstances, but the project demonstrates my ability to manage design, development, and deployment workflow, combining creativity and technical skills.

Kinetik – Interactive Web Experience

This project showcases my creative direction, animation and front-end development skills through a brand I designed called Kinetik - an artsy and eccentric concept focused on dynamic visual interaction.

I designed the logo, built the entire website from scratch, and used this project as an opportunity to experiment with animations and interactivity across the layout. The main inspiration came from a website I discovered featuring a horizontal scroll effect - and I knew I had to recreate it in my own way.

Kinetik became my personal playground for exploring motion, creative coding, and brand identity - a project where I could freely combine art, code, and movement into one cohesive digital experience.

Arta Teh – Construction Company Website

This project showcases the complete visual identity and web design for the construction company Arta Teh. I designed the logo and created a modern, professional one-page website that reflects the company’s reliability, precision, and aesthetic values.

The design was fully developed in Figma, focusing on a clean layout, strong geometric elements, and visual hierarchy that highlights the company’s services and projects. Each section — from the hero area to the contact form — was carefully structured to communicate trust and professionalism through color, typography, and composition.

This project represents a combination of my skills in brand design, layout composition, and user experience, resulting in a coherent digital identity for Arta Teh.

What's Next – Branding & Web Design Concept

During an internship in Spain, I worked on the logo and website design for What’s Next, a company focused on parties and events. The goal was to create a modern, energetic look that appeals to a young, lively audience.

The logo features a forward-pointing arrow, symbolizing progress, movement, and excitement, which aligns with the brand’s fun and dynamic identity. The website design, created in Figma, uses bold typography, vibrant colors, and playful geometric elements to guide users through the content while keeping the overall feel fresh and engaging.

This project demonstrates how a cohesive visual identity—from logo to website—can capture the spirit of a brand and attract its target audience effectively.
